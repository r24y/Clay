Welcome to Clay!

Clay is an OS intended to replace the Kobo's default system. While nickel is pretty great, there are a few things it does not support.

  • To-do lists
  • Calendar
  • Email
  • Advanced WPA2 authentication
  • Custom screensavers
  • And more...

There's no reason why the device should not be capable of doing these things... and in fact it is capable! I'm just unlocking these capabilities so everyone can take advantage of them.

Ryan Muller, December 2012
